Buffalo Chicken Dip

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. Mix the butter, Frank's Hot Sauce, shredded chicken, cheese, cream cheese, and ranch in a baking dish
  3. Bake for 20 minutes
  4. Remove from oven, garnish, and let cool for 1 minute
